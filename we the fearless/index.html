Lives Transformed

1000+ Youths involved

We are blessed to have over 1000 youths actively participating in our events, which are designed to inspire, educate, and empower young Christians. Our events include dynamic worship sessions, impactful sermons, engaging workshops, and various outreach activities. These gatherings not only foster spiritual growth and strengthen faith, but also build a sense of community among the youth.

20+ Partners

We are incredibly grateful for the support of over 20 sponsors who generously contribute to our mission. These sponsors, ranging from local businesses to larger organizations, provide essential resources that enable us to hold impactful events, carry out mission trips, and support our charity work.

1000+ Lives Transformed

We are humbled and grateful to have witnessed the transformation of over 1000 lives through our missions and events. These transformations are a testament to the power of faith, community, and dedicated service. Our missions and events provide opportunities for individuals to experience spiritual growth, receive much-needed support, and find a sense of belonging.

10+ Countries

Our organization is blessed to have a global reach, impacting over 10 countries across Africa and the world. Our missions and events extend to various regions, bringing hope, support, and the message of God’s love to diverse communities. From remote villages in Africa to urban centers across continents, our dedicated teams work tirelessly to address local needs, provide humanitarian aid, and share the Gospel.

Our Mission

To disciple and empower young people to walk in unwavering faith, live courageously for Christ, and become beacons of hope and transformation through evangelism and spiritual maturity.

Our Annual Fearless Camp
Welcome to our Annual Fearless Camp — a week-long, faith-filled journey designed to inspire, empower, and connect! This is not just any camp; it’s a life-changing experience where you’ll encounter the Holy Spirit, build meaningful connections with other youth, and take part in activities that leave a lasting impact. From transformative workshops to thrilling games, exciting events, and a memorable dinner night, there’s something for everyone.
